Training

Session 1

  • Theory(Intro to the wind and power)
  • Training kite
  • Set up the kite
  • Safety systems
  • First piloting

Session 2

  • Flying kite with one hand
  • Lunching and landing
  • Walking with the kite
  • Body dragging in the water
  • Flying kite in power zone
  • Relunch the kite from the water

Session 3

  • Upwind body drag (one handed)
  • Body dragging with the board.
  • Self-rescure and pack down
  • Water start theory
  • Stady pull with the kite
  • Water start with both directions

Session 4

  • Water start
  • Riding in both directions
  • Gaining speed on the board
  • Right of way rules
  • Upwind riding

About

Kitesurfing training in Hurghada (Egypt)

We are the Kite Royal Academy Kitesk School, we offer kiteboarding training and equipment rental on the spot of the beautiful beach of the new Titanic Royal hotel. We have perfect conditions for training and freeride - the sandbanks of the Red Sea and constant winds. There is all necessary equipment, as well as storage. At your disposal will be the newest kites Cabrina Moto 2019. Experienced instructors will help you to easily master this amazing sport in the shortest possible time. At the end of training, we issue an IKO certificate.
