About

Traders Fair attracts the whole financial world under one roof during a single day!

The geography is really huge and covers a great number of regions in South East Asia, Europe, China etc.

Numerous free speaking seminars and workshops, top financial companies, the best trading experts from all over the world are sharing out their experience about foreign exchange, stocks, futures and options markets. The Fair program is full of entertainments, lucky draws, fantastic prizes, live performances. Traders awards ceremony is a main part of this project and holds annually to award the Best from the Best companies in the industry.

The event is produced by FINEXPO, a huge event producing company with over 18 years of experience!

FINEXPO has connected over 30 000 traders, investors and financial advisors and more than 3 000 financial companies and brokers from Forex, stock, option and cryptocurrency markets from all around the world including Malaysia, Indonesia, Singapore, Thailand, Philippines, China, Kazakhstan, Ukraine, Cyprus, Slovakia, Latvia and Russia.
